About me

My name is Michael Gennari, and I am in my final year of a Bachelor of Science degree in mathematical physics with co-operative education at the University of Waterloo in Ontario, Canada. My interests currently include the nuclear many-body problem, ab initio nuclear theory, low-energy quantum chromodyanmics (QCD), chiral effective field theory, particle physics, group theory and machine learning.
